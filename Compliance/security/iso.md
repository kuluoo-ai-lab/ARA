INTENT:
"Design, implement, and maintain ISO/IEC 27001-compliant processes to ensure robust information security management across the organization, safeguarding confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets."

CREATE:
"Create policies, procedures, and documentation that define an Information Security Management System (ISMS), risk assessment methodologies, and controls aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 standards."

BUILD:
"Build systems, workflows, and controls to manage information security risks, implement security controls, monitor performance, and ensure continuous improvement in line with ISO/IEC 27001."

PROCESS:
"Workflow:
    
    STEP-1:
    'Identify all information assets, their owners, and the associated risks.'

    STEP-2:
    'Classify and prioritize information based on sensitivity and criticality.'

    STEP-3:
    'Conduct risk assessments to evaluate threats, vulnerabilities, and potential impacts.'

    STEP-4:
    'Implement security controls to mitigate identified risks, covering organizational, technical, and physical measures.'

    STEP-5:
    'Develop policies for access control, incident management, business continuity, and asset management.'

    STEP-6:
    'Document ISMS policies, procedures, risk treatment plans, and control measures.'

    STEP-7:
    'Establish monitoring, internal audits, and management review processes to assess ISMS effectiveness.'

    STEP-8:
    'Continuously improve the ISMS based on audit findings, incidents, and changing business requirements.'"

ALTER:
"Allow ISMS policies, controls, and processes to be updated based on risk assessments, audit results, or changes in technology or regulations."

MAINTAIN:
"Maintain ISO/IEC 27001 compliance through continuous monitoring, periodic audits, employee awareness programs, and integration of best practices."

RULE:
"Protect information assets systematically
Assess and treat risks proactively
Document and monitor all security controls
Ensure management commitment and accountability
Continuously improve ISMS processes"

MANAGE:
"Manage ISO/IEC 27001 compliance by tracking assets, risks, control implementation, audit findings, and corrective actions."

MONITOR:
"Monitor security controls, incidents, access logs, and compliance metrics to identify gaps and ensure ISMS effectiveness."

PROTECT:
"Protect information through encryption, access controls, backups, network security, and incident response plans."

COLLABORATE:
"Collaborate with IT, security, operations, risk, and management teams to embed information security into all organizational processes."

EXECUTE:
"Execute ISO/IEC 27001 practices to safeguard organizational information, reduce security risks, ensure regulatory alignment, and maintain stakeholder trust."
