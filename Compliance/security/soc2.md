INTENT:
"Design, implement, and maintain SOC 2-compliant processes to ensure that systems managing sensitive customer data meet the Trust Services Criteria of security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy."

CREATE:
"Create policies, procedures, and documentation that define internal controls over system operations, data handling, and risk management aligned with SOC 2 requirements."

BUILD:
"Build systems, workflows, and controls to ensure secure, reliable, and confidential handling of customer data while demonstrating compliance with SOC 2 criteria."

PROCESS:
"Workflow:
    
    STEP-1:
    'Identify and document all systems, applications, and services that handle sensitive customer data.'

    STEP-2:
    'Map SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria to organizational processes and controls.'

    STEP-3:
    'Implement security controls including access management, change management, monitoring, and incident response.'

    STEP-4:
    'Ensure availability controls such as system redundancy, backup, and disaster recovery.'

    STEP-5:
    'Implement processing integrity measures to guarantee accurate and complete processing of data.'

    STEP-6:
    'Safeguard confidentiality and privacy of customer information through encryption, data masking, and secure transmission.'

    STEP-7:
    'Document all policies, controls, procedures, and evidence for audit readiness.'

    STEP-8:
    'Conduct periodic internal audits, gap assessments, and remediation of deficiencies.'"

ALTER:
"Allow SOC 2 policies, controls, and processes to evolve with new technologies, regulatory updates, or operational changes. Document all updates."

MAINTAIN:
"Maintain SOC 2 compliance through ongoing monitoring, employee training, control testing, and continuous process improvement."

RULE:
"Ensure security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy
Document all controls and evidence
Regularly test and improve controls
Address incidents promptly
Maintain management accountability for compliance"

MANAGE:
"Manage SOC 2 compliance by tracking control implementation, audit results, risk assessments, and remediation plans."

MONITOR:
"Monitor system access, data handling, operational performance, and security incidents proactively to detect and mitigate compliance gaps."

PROTECT:
"Protect customer data using encryption, access restrictions, secure infrastructure, and incident response plans."

COLLABORATE:
"Collaborate with IT, security, operations, legal, and audit teams to ensure SOC 2 controls are effectively integrated into all processes."

EXECUTE:
"Execute SOC 2 compliance practices to maintain system trustworthiness, reduce operational and reputational risk, and demonstrate commitment to data protection."
