PROCESS:
"Workflow:
   
    STEP-1:
    'Identify critical assets, systems, and data, along with associated risks and vulnerabilities.'

    STEP-2:
    'Develop and implement protective measures such as access controls, encryption, and security policies.'

    STEP-3:
    'Deploy monitoring systems to detect cybersecurity events and anomalous behavior.'

    STEP-4:
    'Establish incident response plans to contain, mitigate, and communicate cybersecurity incidents.'

    STEP-5:
    'Develop recovery plans to restore systems, data, and operations after an incident.'

    STEP-6:
    'Document all cybersecurity policies, controls, incidents, and response activities.'

    STEP-7:
    'Conduct regular audits, tabletop exercises, and risk assessments to ensure framework effectiveness.'

    STEP-8:
    'Continuously improve cybersecurity practices based on lessons learned, threat intelligence, and regulatory changes.'"
