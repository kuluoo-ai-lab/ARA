INTENT:
"Design, implement, and maintain CCPA/CPRA-compliant processes to ensure the protection of personal information of California residents, upholding consumer privacy rights and meeting regulatory obligations."

CREATE:
"Create policies, procedures, and documentation that ensure transparency, consumer control, and responsible handling of personal information, including disclosure of data collection, sale, and sharing practices."

BUILD:
"Build systems, workflows, and controls that enforce CCPA/CPRA principles, including consumer rights management, opt-out mechanisms, and accountability for third-party data sharing."

PROCESS:
"Workflow:
  
    STEP-1:
    'Identify all personal information collected, used, sold, or shared about California residents.'

    STEP-2:
    'Classify data by type, sensitivity, and purpose of processing.'

    STEP-3:
    'Provide clear and accessible privacy notices and disclosures at the point of collection.'

    STEP-4:
    'Implement mechanisms for consumers to exercise their rights: access, deletion, opt-out of sale, and non-discrimination.'

    STEP-5:
    'Develop processes to verify consumer requests and respond within regulatory timelines.'

    STEP-6:
    'Track third-party sharing and establish contracts ensuring compliance with CCPA/CPRA obligations.'

    STEP-7:
    'Document data flows, consumer requests, and response actions.'

    STEP-8:
    'Conduct regular audits and updates to privacy practices to remain compliant.'"

ALTER:
"Allow policies, workflows, and consumer-facing processes to be updated as regulations evolve or business practices change. Ensure updates are transparent and well-documented."

MAINTAIN:
"Maintain CCPA/CPRA compliance through continuous monitoring, staff training, periodic privacy reviews, and consumer rights management."

RULE:
"Provide clear privacy notices
Respect consumer rights promptly
Limit data sharing to necessary purposes
Document all actions and decisions
Ensure non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights"

MANAGE:
"Manage CCPA/CPRA compliance by tracking consumer requests, data inventories, third-party agreements, and internal audit results."

MONITOR:
"Monitor data collection, sharing activities, request fulfillment timelines, and regulatory updates to proactively identify compliance gaps."

PROTECT:
"Protect personal information using encryption, access controls, secure storage, and contractual safeguards for third parties. Maintain breach response procedures."

COLLABORATE:
"Collaborate with legal, IT, data privacy officers, operations, marketing, and external vendors to ensure consistent compliance across all touchpoints."
