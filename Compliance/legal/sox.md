INTENT:
"Design, implement, and maintain SOX-compliant processes to ensure accurate financial reporting, corporate governance, and internal controls for publicly traded companies in the US."

CREATE:
"Create policies, procedures, and documentation that govern financial reporting, internal controls, auditing, and compliance monitoring to meet Sarbanes-Oxley Act requirements."

BUILD:
"Build systems, workflows, and controls that enforce SOX principles, including segregation of duties, transaction monitoring, approval workflows, and audit trails."

PROCESS:
"Workflow:
    
    STEP-1:
    'Identify all financial reporting processes and critical controls impacting financial statements.'

    STEP-2:
    'Assess risk of material misstatement and establish control objectives.'

    STEP-3:
    'Implement internal controls over financial reporting (ICFR) including authorization, verification, and reconciliation steps.'

    STEP-4:
    'Document processes, control activities, and key financial data flows.'

    STEP-5:
    'Conduct periodic testing of controls to validate effectiveness.'

    STEP-6:
    'Establish issue remediation workflows for control deficiencies.'

    STEP-7:
    'Maintain audit trails and supporting evidence for internal and external auditors.'

    STEP-8:
    'Regularly review and update SOX controls in response to process changes or audit findings.'"

ALTER:
"Allow SOX policies, processes, and controls to be updated as business processes evolve or regulations change. Ensure all changes are documented and communicated to stakeholders."

MAINTAIN:
"Maintain SOX compliance through ongoing monitoring, employee training, internal audits, and collaboration with external auditors."

RULE:
"Ensure accurate, complete, and timely financial reporting
Implement robust internal controls
Segregate duties to reduce risk of fraud
Document all transactions and controls
Continuously review and improve control environment"

MANAGE:
"Manage SOX compliance by tracking control ownership, testing results, remediation plans, and audit documentation."

MONITOR:
"Monitor financial processes, control performance, and audit findings proactively to prevent material misstatements."

PROTECT:
"Protect financial data and systems from unauthorized access, manipulation, or loss. Maintain secure backups and access controls."

COLLABORATE:
"Collaborate with finance, accounting, IT, internal audit, and external audit teams to ensure consistent SOX compliance."

EXECUTE:
"Execute SOX compliance practices to safeguard financial integrity, build investor trust, and reduce regulatory risk."
